NOW That’s What I Call Music

This year is the 20th anniversary and there have been 67 US albums since 1998. The songs included were the most “now” songs when each edition was released, but which album remains the most “now” now? Limit it to just albums released in 2008 or earlier to give a good gap between then and now (that gives us 29). Take the track list from each album and use the Spotify API to get the current popularity of a track. Maybe even use EchoNest points to find the NOW album that sounds most similar to today’s hits.
